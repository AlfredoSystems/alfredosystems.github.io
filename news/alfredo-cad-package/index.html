The Alfredo CAD Package

Written by Jacob Williams

You may have noticed we have SolidWorks CAD of many of our parts on their product pages. While this is useful if you just need one or two parts, we figure we should have the full CAD package available in a more central location. Here is the ZIP with every part (and a version with STEP files too for non-SolidWorks users)

  • Alfredo CAD Package SLDPRT
  • Alfredo CAD Package STEP
Here is a list of parts included in this package:
  • 9V Battery
  • Alfredo NoU2
  • Flywheel Motor
  • N20 Motor
  • Power Cell
  • TT motor
  • TT wheel
